Students for Sustainable Action is a student association in which I play an active role as a graphic designer and board member. Most of the graphic work I have done for them is in relation to social media. Facebook banners, instagram stories and others. We raise awareness about social, environmental and economic problems on campus.


The global climate strike event was one of our most popular and successful events we've had. The vector illustration of a turtle I created for an event day during the University's sustainability week. The theme was Sustainable Cities and Environments, we collaborated with Jönköping University Sustainability and Diversity Network.
