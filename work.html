Work


Most of my work is based on research. I take a few days before I start designing anything to read and think about the subject. This helps me understand the scope of the work. I devide the task into smaller chunks and create a plan. If I have the needed resources I start executing the plan. The way you approach a problem is crutial, if the preparation and planning have gone wrong, this will affect the delivered work immensely.

Inspiration can be drawn from anything and anyone. We are all creators and contributors to the world surrounding us.

Personal projects I call "passion projects", they help me distract myself if I am stuck on a task. By switching between tasks I am able to gain different perspectives and clear my headspace. I believe that there should be balance between client projects and personal projects. Nothing good will come out if you keep banging your head against a wall on a client project, we all need fresh air sometimes.



Balance is crucial to everything.
